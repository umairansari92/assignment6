Generative AI

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that can learn from and mimic large amounts of data to create content such as text, images, music, videos, code, and more, based on inputs or prompts¹. Generative AI can not only create new content, but also improve existing content by rewriting, summarizing, optimizing, and generating ideas.

One of the most popular and powerful examples of generative AI is Jesper chatbot, an AI writing assistant that works like a “conversational chatbot”². You can simply ask questions or give commands and Jesper chat will help you create engaging content quickly.

What is Jesper chatbot and how does it work?

Jesper chatbot is a brand-spanking-new conversational chatbot that was launched by Jasper AI, a company that provides AI tools for enterprise marketing teams. Jasper chatbot is powered by Jasper AI Engine, which interoperates across the latest models and supports multimodality for text and images.

Jesper chatbot has learned from billions of articles, forums, video transcripts, and content published on the web. This gives Jesper the ability to have a conversation about complex subjects with superb detail². Jesper chatbot also understands and writes in 30+ languages, so you can chat in your native tongue, then watch the AI respond back to you in whatever language you would like.

To use Jesper chatbot, you just need to start a chat with Jasper by asking a question or stating a command. For example, you can ask Jasper to write a blog post, summarize a transcript, write a Facebook ad, explain a concept, help you write a short story, generate ideas for headlines, and more². Jasper will then reply with relevant and original content that you can use or edit as you wish.

What are the benefits and applications of Jesper chatbot?

Jesper chatbot can help you with various content creation tasks, such as:

  • Generating fresh ideas and getting inspiration.
  • Polishing your content before you publish.
  • Scaling up content while maintaining high-quality.
  • Developing on-brand content with the assistance of AI.
  • Turning a single brief or piece of content into a multichannel campaign in seconds.
  • Developing beautiful supporting imagery with just a few lines of text.
  • Getting AI recommendations for how to improve your content performance.
  • Directly publishing AI-optimized content to your platform of choice.

Jesper chatbot can also help you have fun and explore what AI can do, such as writing poems, telling jokes, making a script for your skit, or even generating your daily horoscope.

Why is Jesper chatbot better than other generative AI tools?

Jesper chatbot is not just another generative AI tool, but a friendly and helpful collaborator that can supercharge your imagination, boost your productivity, and bring your ideas to life². Jesper chatbot has some unique features that make it stand out from other generative AI tools, such as:

  • Familiar design that is accessible and easy to use. Interacting with artificial intelligence can feel difficult and overwhelming, but Jasper chats conversational interface breaks down those barriers while keeping things simple and fun.
  • Remembers what you said earlier, giving it better context. Jasper chat is excellent at following along a storyline and referencing information you or Jasper have said previously in the chat.
  • Feels like your brand, not generic. Jasper chat can infuse your content with your brand and product positioning, voice and style guide, and company strategy.
  • Writes factually about your business. Jasper chat can root your content in company strategy, competitive intelligence, and audience research.
  • Knows up-to-date information and cites sources. Jasper chat can access the latest information from the web and provide references for its content.
  • Built for marketing performance. Jasper chat can help you optimize your content for SEO, conversions, and engagement.
  • Goes everywhere with browser extension. Jasper chat can integrate with your favorite tools and platforms with a simple browser extension.
  • Collaborate with your team and manage users. Jasper chat can help you streamline reviews, project management, and publishing with your team.
